The New Vice Chancellor of the World United Colleges of Bishops for Africa Region

H. E. Bishop Prof. Amb. Michael Nwokolo, PhD

A BRIEF PROFILE OF THE VICE CHANCELLOR

BISHOP, PROF, MICHAEL NWOKOLO; Ph.D., TH.D., PGD, M.SC., B.SC., CSS, OSHS, PFSO, FNIIS, FOSHA, FCIPM, FCFE, FABS, FIBTM, FCFN, FIGRLT, FCILM, FIGAN, MNIM, MISOM, MIACP.

CHARTERED MEDIATOR

VISO – Veteran International Security Organisation, Swittzerland.
3rd Commandment General Chaplains Fellowship of Nigeria (CFN)
Member…. World United Colleges of Bishops
Member – United Nations Chaplaincy and Rescue Mission
Nigerian Director of Protocol – United Nations Chaplaincy and Rescue Missions
President/Founder Institute of Public Safety and Security in Work Environment

He is a counselor, Ordained Reverend Dr., Evangelist, Author, Administrator, Researcher, Sociologist, Theologist, Graphologist, Forensic Fingerprint Expert . United Nations Ambassador International Awardee and Delta State Icon Merit Awardee. Also Ika First Achievers Award. He is a graduate of Public Administration (B.Sc) from Adekunle Ajasin University Akungba, Ondo State, a Post Graduate Diploma holder in Business Administration, Lagos. M.Sc in Sociology University of Lagos, PhD in Sociology Green Hills University, Denmark, Professor – Edexcel University Benin Republic.

He is also a social worker and cares for people. He is married with three children who are graduates of various universities.

He was Consecrated Bishop on 4th December 2022 by World United Colleges of Bishops (WUCB), through His Eminence Archbishop Prof. Nathan Tor Luga.
Additionally, His Lordship Bishop, Amb., Prof. Michael Nwokolo was appointed the Vice Chancellor of the World United Colleges of Bishops for Africa Region on 4th December,2022.
