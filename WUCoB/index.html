ABOUT US

We are a family of like minded men and women networking together and dedicated to bring peace and prosperity to Humanity, through the gospel of God the Creator, encouraging and training the members to fulfill their God given assignments. WUCB serves as one of the Agents of the United Nations. WUCB is partnered with United Nations University, UNUGP USA, The American University, AUGP USA, KBF Centre for international Studies, Ghana, International Christian University, Lagos, Nigeria & Overseas, and other International and National Commissions and Institutions.
Our Human Rights and Charity activities of the United Nations Human Rights are to fulfill the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals-UN SDGs. WUCB is set to eradicate poverty, injustice and bring world peace and prosperity, social empowerment through our programs and activities in consonant with UN SDGs.
