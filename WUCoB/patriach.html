The Patriarch




Brief Profile of the Patriarch

Dr. Madhu Krishan is an eminent social reformer and activist, Environmentalist, Scientist, Educator, Peace builder, Human Rights Activist and Humanitarian Works Expert. He is the founder and Chairman AUGP and Rector of the University for Global Peace. He is the chairman UN Global Development Organization.

He is also the "Patriarch for Life" of the World United Colleges of Bishops - WUCB, USA and The patriarch field Marshal of UN Chaplaincy and Rescue Mission, an Arm of the United Nations University - UNUGP, USA.
