WUCB in Brief

WUCB serves as a resource in both fellowship and faith based development. By Fellowship as stated, we mean brothers of like passion coming together to encourage, build and enhance one another.
Our coming together is to provide a sense of belonging to a whole, while yet remaining independent individually. WUCB also serves as a faith group coverage for its members. In as much as WUCB does not govern any of its members' organizations or ministries, members should however live the life representative of the Creator, the Messiah and above reproach. Therefore, World United Colleges of Bishops (WUCB) and its leadership strongly believe, teach and exemplify a high standard of integrity, character and moral excellence.
WUCB also license Organizations, trains ministers, ordain and license ministers, consecrate, license and install Bishops, Archbishops, etc.


COLLEGE ACADEMIC MATTERS

We offer world accredited and recognized Degrees Certificates that are acceptable in all Nations of the world through our partner KBF Center for International Studies, Ghana, affiliated to City University Cambodia, California University USA, London University, UK and other 23 World recognized Universities.
We train and equip organizations and ministers to suit in or to be in consonant with the moving world technology and system, so that they can effectively fulfill their visions. The college has Certificate and Diploma Programs in:

  • Finance/Administration

  • Leadership/Administration

  • Ministry

  • Theology
